About us

Hand-drawn stories, screen-printed by hand

TOTEM is an independent design brand focused on handmade bandanas and accessories, featuring our own hand-drawn and screen-printed illustrations.

Since 2017, we have been creating pieces that carry stories, symbols, and a sense of individuality.
